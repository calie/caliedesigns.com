Community Mobile App

The online community app gives Higher Logic's users a simple way to engage with their communities on-the-go. Features include:

  • A filterable feed to browse latest content
  • Create, comment and interact with discussion posts
  • View and RSVP to events
  • Upload photos and files to the library
  • Explore blog posts
  • Manage profiles

My role: we hired a 3rd party development company for this work and my role involved scope definition and UX direction. I designed the logo and branding for the project.
